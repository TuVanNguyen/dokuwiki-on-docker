# Self-hypnosis

## Types of Frames

### Positive Framing
  * building, strengthening, and maintaining positive suggestions
  * think, feel, behave differently
  * better for beginners to start here

### Negative Framing
  * breaking or untangling suggestions ("dehypnotizing")
  * address underlying problems
