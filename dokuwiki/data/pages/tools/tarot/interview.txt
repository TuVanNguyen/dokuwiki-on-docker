====== Interview Spreads ======

Applications:
  * Get to know a new tarot deck 
  * Inquire about a new object that you want to bring into the household (like a job interview)

Questions:
  - Tell me about yourself
  - What are your strengths?
  - What are your weaknesses?
  - What can I learn from you?
  - How can I best work with you?
  - What will come out of our relationship?
